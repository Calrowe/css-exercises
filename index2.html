Brynolfs quest

You are the fierce viking warrior Brynolf, son of Ulfrick, and you
are on one of many boats raiding the coasts of England. You jump
ashore with you your fellow mates at yet another monastery that
looks like an easy raid. You first walk towards it and after they
noticed you and their bells start to chime you all start to run
with a roar of combat.
Halfway there arrows from the forest nearby starts to rain down on you
and your chieftain yells:
"Quick! Change course towards the forest! Shields in front"
You all run towards the bowmen and soldiers at the forestline but half-
way there suddenly arrows starts to rain over you from behind.
"Dammit! Its a trap! Keep the course!", chieftain Olaf screams to his
men. When you finally reach the forest over half of your fellow soldiers
has allready fallen from the arrows. The english soldiers are now more
than thrice as many any the battle stands in their favor.
An arrow just misses your head. You look around and you find the bowman.
He quickly shoots down two of your friends.
"If I am only going to slay one of them, it should be him", you think
for youself. As if he had read your mind the bowman quickly dissappears
into the forest.

What do you do?

  1. FLEE the battle and run for the boats
  2. CHASE the bowman into the woods
  3. STAY and defend your comrades in the battle
